Our FOCUS


NextGen Tech Initiatives is an innovative start-up engaged in the challenging field of sustainable mobility. Our
group activities include automotive services, promoting use of electric vehicles, offering cost-effective design
and development of automotive sub-systems.
We seek collaborations/alliances to create sustainable mobility solutions for urban areas and are willing to
participate and contribute towards policy making and defining parameters for the nascent EV industry in India.

Electric mobility is at the core of our innovation and research. We strive towards providing cost-
effective and workable solutions for the developing electric vehicle industry and market.
